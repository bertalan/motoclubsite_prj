Our History

Born in 2010 from a group of friends passionate about motorcycles and travel, MotoItalia has grown to become the largest Italian community dedicated to motorcycle touring.

We believe that traveling by motorcycle is the best way to discover the beauties of our country: from the Alpine peaks to medieval villages, from wild coasts to gentle hills, every road tells a story.

Our mission is to share this passion, creating unforgettable routes and a community where every motorcyclist can feel at home.
