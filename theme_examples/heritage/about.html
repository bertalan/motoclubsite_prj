Our Story

Founded in 1985 by a group of visionaries, The Society was established with a singular purpose: to create a distinguished community where tradition meets excellence, and where members can forge lasting connections.

Over four decades, we have grown from a small gathering of like-minded individuals to a thriving organization with eight chapters and over 350 members worldwide. Our commitment to preserving heritage while embracing innovation has been the cornerstone of our success.
