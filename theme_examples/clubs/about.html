The History

Forty Years of Passion

Born in 1985 from a meeting of five friends united by their passion for two wheels, EagleRiders has grown to become the reference point for motorcyclists in the region. Ours is a story made of roads traveled together, friendships that last a lifetime, values that are passed down.

Every Sunday morning, for forty years, our members gather to share breakfasts, stories and of course miles. This is the true essence of EagleRiders: an extended family that lives motorcycling not as a means of transport, but as a philosophy of life.

Timeline

Key Moments

1985

Founding

Five friends found the club in a provincial garage. The first rally counts 12 participants.

1995

100 Members

The club reaches 100 members. The first official headquarters is inaugurated.

2005

FMI Recognition

The Italian Motorcycle Federation officially recognizes the club.

2020

New Headquarters

Inauguration of the new headquarters with museum, social workshop and event hall.
